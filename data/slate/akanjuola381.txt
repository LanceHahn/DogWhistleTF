Wakanda Forever carries on the story that thrilled fans of the 2018 blockbuster Black Panther. On today’s episode of A Word, Jason Johnson is joined by Evan Narcisse. He’s one of the comic book writers whose work—including the Marvel Black Panther Wakanda Atlas—helped flesh out Wakandan mythology in the years since the original film debuted. Narcisse talks about how the Afrofuturistic utopia inspired him, and how he and other creators hope to build its legacy.Guest: Writer Evan NarcissePodcast production by Kristie Taiwo-Makanjuola.
