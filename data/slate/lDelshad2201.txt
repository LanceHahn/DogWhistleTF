Last Friday, a surging crowd killed at least eight people and left hundreds injured at Travis Scott’s music festival Astroworld in Houston. Public uproar over the needless deaths has placed responsibility at the rapper’s feet—and at those of police officers who failed to intervene and shut the show down.How did Scott’s signature “raging” spill over into a mass casualty event?
