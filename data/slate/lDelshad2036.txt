Michigan state Sen. Rosemary Bayer has been fighting for years to get safe storage laws on the books in her home state. In the wake of the shooting at Oxford High School, a school that sits in her district, her mission has become that much more urgent.Guest: Rosemary Bayer is a state senator in Michigan. She represents the northern suburbs of Detroit.Podcast production by Mary Wilson, Danielle Hewitt, Elena Schwartz, and Carmel Delshad.
