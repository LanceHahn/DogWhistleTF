Back in March, Texas Governor Greg Abbott sent a couple hundred members of the Texas National Guard to the U.S.-Mexico border to counteract what he called President Biden’s “open border policies.” Almost a year later, around 10,000 troops are stationed there at Abbott’s command. Many guardsmen have had to upend their lives for a mission that seems to have little purpose.Guest: Davis Winkie, staff reporter for Army Times.Podcast production by Mary Wilson, Danielle Hewitt, Elena Schwartz, and Carmel Delshad.
