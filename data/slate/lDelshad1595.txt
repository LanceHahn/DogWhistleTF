No one knows what’s going on in Russian leader Vladimir Putin’s head—and while that’s a worrisome idea during the best of times, it’s an especially grim one during a war of his own creation. Putin is increasingly isolated, away from his inner circle and the oligarchs who once had some influence with him.Guest: Ben Judah is a senior fellow at the Atlantic Council and the author of This Is London and Fragile Empire: How Russia Fell In and Out of Love With Vladimir Putin.Podcast production by Mary Wilson, Danielle Hewitt, Elena Schwartz, and Carmel Delshad.
