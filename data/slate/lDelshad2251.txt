India Walton won the Democratic primary for mayor of Buffalo, New York, in June. Her victory put her on track to become the first socialist mayor in the city’s history, and the first nationwide in more than half a century. The only problem is, her opponent—current Mayor Byron Brown—refuses to bow out, and is waging a well-funded write-in campaign in the hopes of keeping his post.How has a self-styled socialist gotten so close to running Buffalo?
