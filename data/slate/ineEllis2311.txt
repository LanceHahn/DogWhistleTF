Whether the issue is COVID policy or police violence, Black athletes can’t run, jump, or score their way out of the racial power dynamics of major sports. On today’s episode of A Word, veteran sportswriter and author Howard Bryant joins Jason Johnson to talk about the intersection of sports and race. They discuss the controversy over basketball star Kyrie Irving’s vaccine resistance, former NFL coach Jon Gruden’s offensive emails, and Colin Kaepernick’s endless campaign to return to the football field.Guest: Howard Bryant, veteran sports writer, commentator, and author of several books including The Heritage: Black Athletes, a Divided America, and the Politics of Patriotism.Podcast production by Jasmine Ellis.
